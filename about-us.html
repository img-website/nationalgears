Company Overview

We Engineer Reliability

Welcome to National Gears! Founded by Late Mr. Ramesh Chand Gupta in the year of 1997 with a passion for precision engineering, National Gears has established itself as a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality gears and transmission solutions.

Our journey began with a commitment to excellence and innovation, and over the years, we have built a reputation for delivering top-notch products and exceptional customer service. We don't just manufacture components; we engineer reliability.
